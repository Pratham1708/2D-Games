Welcome to 2-D Games!

Explore our collection of classic 2D games, built with love and care. From the nostalgic Flappy Bird to the strategic Tic Tac Toe, we've got something for everyone. Click on a game to start playing!

Flappy Bird

A classic arcade-style game where you control a bird that must navigate through obstacles by flapping its wings.

Memory Card Game

A puzzle game that challenges your memory and concentration. Match all the cards in as few moves as possible.

Tic Tac Toe

A strategic game of chance where two players, X and O, take turns marking a 3x3 grid. Get three of your marks in a row to win.

Rock Paper Scissors

A simple yet fun game of chance. Play against the computer and try to win by choosing the right hand.
